Homeowner Goals

Find out how much you need to save each month and the salary you need to buy a starter home based on your current age and where you live.

The maximum difference between your current age and the age you want to buy can be 33 years.

How it works

To work out starter home house prices in each area we looked at each area's median house price on Zillow and then multiplied this by 2/3. We then looked at how much house prices have changed from 1991-2024 on the Federal Housing Finance Agency and projected this forward to find out starter house prices from 2026 - 2058.
