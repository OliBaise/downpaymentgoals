Downpayment Goals

Find out how much money you need to save each month to buy your first home. The maximum differene between your current age and the age you want to buy at is 32 years.

How It Works

We looked at median home prices in all the areas listed on Zillow. We then multiplied this by 2/3 to get an estimate for a typical "starter home"

We then got house price increases from 1991 to 2024 and projected these forward to work out how much these homes would cost from 2026 - 2058. These come from the Federal Housing Financing Agency
